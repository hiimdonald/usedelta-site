How it works

Coaching built around your data

Delta reads your biometric data, scores what it means for today, and delivers a clear morning brief — so you always know what to do.

  • Aggregates all your health data in one place

    Connects to Oura Ring, Apple Health, Peloton, and more — pulling sleep, HRV, readiness, workouts, and activity into a unified daily picture.

  • Scores your recovery, training load, and strain

    Proprietary algorithms compute daily scores for recovery, acute and chronic training load, and session strain — contextualized to your baseline.

  • Delivers a personalized morning brief

    Each morning, Delta generates a concise coaching brief: what your numbers mean, whether to push or recover, and what to focus on today.

  • Tracks nutrition via natural language

    Log meals conversationally — just describe what you ate. Delta parses macros and calories and keeps a running daily tally alongside your other metrics.

Privacy

Your data stays yours

Delta collects health and biometric data solely to generate your personal coaching briefs. Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest, stored on infrastructure hosted by Supabase (AWS). It is never sold, rented, or shared with advertisers or other third parties.

The only external processor that touches your data is Anthropic (Claude AI), which receives anonymized daily metric summaries — not raw health records — to generate brief text. You can disconnect any integration or delete your account and all associated data at any time.
